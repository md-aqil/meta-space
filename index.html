Token Economics

META tokens will be available for purchase during the public sale and will be listed on major cryptocurrency exchanges for trading. The platform will implement a buyback and burn program to reduce the total supply of META tokens over time, creating a deflationary model that will increase the token's value. As the platform grows and expands, the demand for META tokens is expected to increase, creating a positive feedback loop that benefits both the platform and its users.At MetaSpace,
